Delicious Chocolate Cake

Description

If you spend time eating at least 1 slice of cake, you will become Very Happy!

Reagents

  • 8 Simple Flour
  • 4 Ice Cold Milk
  • 4 Mild Spices
  • 8 Small Egg
  • 3 Mageroyal
  • Flask of Stormwind Tawny

Steps

  1. Mix ingredients in a bowl until thick
  2. Inside a roaring bonfire, bake for 20m
  3. Realize the cake is a lie
  4. Perish
