Feast of Gluttonous Hedonism

Description

Set out a Feast of Gluttonous Hedonism to feed up to 35 people in your raid or party!

Reagents

  • 10 Spinefin Piranha
  • 10 Silvergill Pike
  • 10 Tenebrous Ribs
  • 10 Phantasmal Haunch
  • 5 Elysian Thade
  • 5 Smuggled Azerothian Produce
  • 5 Rich Grazer Milk
  • 5 Lusterwheat Flour

Steps

  1. Place all ingredients in a bubbling cauldron
  2. Slowly bring to a boil
  3. Reduce heat until mixture is cold
  4. Take at least 10 bites
  5. Gain 20 in a random stat for 1 hour!
